# Quick Reader - Version 1.0.0 ⚡

**Official Release: Zen Mode Calibration**

## ✨ Core Features
- **Premium Zen UI**: An immersive, glassmorphic reading environment designed to hide all vault distractions.
- **Stable Anchor System (ORP Centering)**: Mathematical pivot logic that locks the focal letter to the exact center of your display, eliminating eye jitter and increasing reading stability.
- **Dual-Tone Performance Palette**: 
  - **Electric Blue**: Primary interface accents, progress bars, and navigation elements.
  - **Crimson Red**: High-contrast, glow-free reading anchor for maximum focus point sharpness.
- **Direct Visual Calibration**: Instantaneous color changes between words and headings to maximize high-speed recognition without "ghosting" or blurs.

## ⌨️ Control & Navigation
- **Dynamic Speed Tuning**: Use **Up/Down arrow keys** to increase or decrease reading speed in real-time (±25 WPM steps). Changes are persisted to your plugin settings automatically.
- **Structural Nav**: Integrated **Minimap** with heading markers and a hierarchical **Breadcrumb** trail for instant navigation through long documents.
- **Context Expansion**: Press **'C'** while paused to instantly expand the current word into its full surrounding sentence context.
- **Seek Control**: Use **Left/Right arrow keys** to jump backward or forward by 10 words.

## 🧠 Intelligent Reading Logic
- **Micropause Engine**: Integrated timing logic for punctuation, paragraph breaks, and lists.
- **Heading Awareness**: Automatic zoom and color transitions when reaching structural document headings.
- **Callout Support**: Visual separators and specific pauses for Obsidian callouts (admonitions).
- **Smooth Structural Transitions**: Fluid layout shifts for non-text changes (headings, context toggles) while maintaining sharp, direct text clarity.

## 🛠️ Design Philosophy
Quick Reader is built for users who want to push their reading speed to the limit without compromising on comprehension. The **Stable Anchor** and **Dual-Tone** theme are specifically engineered to provide the most stable reading experience possible in Obsidian.
